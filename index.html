The Legendary Punjabi Taste of West Delhi

Located in the busy shopping market of Tagore Garden, **Sardar Ji Punjabi Restaurant** brings the original taste of rustic Punjab to West Delhi. We make our spice blends by hand and use traditional clay tandoors to cook our kebabs and naans.

We are especially popular for our late-night service. When the rest of Delhi sleeps, our ovens are roasting hot to ensure your midnight cravings are satisfied with pure, butter-loaded Punjabi food.
